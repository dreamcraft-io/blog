Privacy Policy for JSON Formatter for Xcode

Last Updated: [MAY 1, 2025]

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application "JSON Formatter for Xcode" (the "Application") for macOS created by dreamcraft (the "Developer").

Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed as an Xcode Source Editor extension to help format JSON files directly within the Xcode environment.

The Application does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or any data from your files or your device.

All JSON processing performed by the Application occurs locally on your device. The content of your JSON files is read by the extension solely for the purpose of formatting or compacting it as per your command, and the modified content replaces the existing content within your Xcode editor session. This data is not sent to the Developer or any third party.

No Data Collection

To be clear, the Application does not:

  • Collect or ask for any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, etc.).
  • Collect any non-personally identifiable information (such as device type, operating system version, usage statistics, etc.).
  • Access, collect, or transmit the content of your files outside of the explicit formatting operations initiated by you within Xcode.
  • Use any third-party analytics or advertising services.
  • Track your activity within the Application or within Xcode.

Permissions

The Application, when enabled as an Xcode Source Editor extension, requires permission to access and modify the content of the active document in Xcode. This permission is solely used to read the JSON content from your file, process it locally, and then update the file content within Xcode based on the command you invoke (e.g., "Format JSON", "Compact JSON"). No data is sent externally.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the Application does not collect any personal information at all, it is inherently compliant with children's privacy regulations such as the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information (which should not be possible with this Application), please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
